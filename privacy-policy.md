# 家盈记 隐私政策 / HomeGain Privacy Policy

*最后更新 / Last updated: 2026-03-27*

---

## 中文版

### 简介

家盈记（HomeGain）是一款完全本地运行的家庭资产管理应用。我们重视您的隐私。

### 数据收集

**家盈记不收集任何用户数据。**

具体而言：
- 不收集个人身份信息（姓名、邮箱、手机号等）
- 不收集财务数据（您录入的所有账户、余额、交易记录均仅存储在您的设备本地）
- 不收集位置信息
- 不收集设备标识符
- 不使用任何第三方数据分析 SDK

### 网络访问

家盈记**不需要网络连接**，不向任何服务器发送数据，不与任何第三方共享数据。

### 数据存储

所有数据以加密形式存储在您的设备本地。数据的备份文件由您自行导出和管理，存储在您指定的位置（如 iCloud Drive、本地文件夹等），我们无法访问这些备份。

### 数据删除

如需删除所有数据，直接在 iOS 上卸载应用即可。卸载后，设备本地的所有数据将被系统清除。

### 联系我们

如有隐私相关问题，请提交 Issue：[GitHub Issues](https://github.com/YuanGuangsheng/homegain-site/issues)

## English Version

### Overview

HomeGain is a fully offline family asset management app. We take your privacy seriously.

### Data Collection

**HomeGain does not collect any user data.**

Specifically:
- No personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- No financial data — all accounts, balances, and transaction records you enter are stored locally on your device only
- No location data
- No device identifiers
- No third-party analytics SDKs

### Network Access

HomeGain **requires no internet connection** and does not transmit any data to any server. No data is shared with any third party.

### Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. Backup files are exported and managed by you, stored wherever you choose (e.g., iCloud Drive, local folder). We have no access to your backups.

### Data Deletion

To delete all your data, simply uninstall the app from your iOS device. The operating system will remove all locally stored app data upon uninstallation.

### Contact

For privacy-related questions, please open an issue: [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/YuanGuangsheng/homegain-site/issues)
